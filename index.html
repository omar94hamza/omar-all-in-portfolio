1. What kind of games do you specialize in?

I specialize in engaging player-centric game design, where gameplay mechanics, narrative, and impactful design take precedence. I create games across different genres, focusing primarily on action, RPG, Simulator and multiplayer experiences.

2. Do you take freelance or contract work?

YES! I am open to freelance and contract opportunities, whether they involve a specific game system, full game design, or something else peculiar where I would love to contribute to your vision.

3. What game engines do you work with?

I'm specialized in Unreal Engine, more on the C++ and Blueprints side. And Unity is also in my toolkit, where I do both 2D and 3D experiences, with mobile development being done with Android Studio.

4. Can you help me out in optimizing my game or app?

For sure! I know all that it takes to optimize: from profiling tools like Unreal Insights to ensuring low loading times and a stable experience regardless of the device.

5. Do you offer any services for game production or project management?

Yes, I offer support on game development and production with the Scrum and Agile methodologies I've become accustomed to. Whether it is project planning, sprint management, or system architecture design, I can help you ease the work.
